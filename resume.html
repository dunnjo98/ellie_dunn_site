Ellie is one half of the hilarious two-woman comedy duo MEL & EL

Hailed as "outrageous" by The New York Times and "hilariously squirm-inducing" by the Village Voice, real-life best friends and NYC cult phenomenon MEL & EL have brought down the house at Joe’s Pub, Ars Nova, Gotham Comedy Club, The Box, Birdland, The Stonewall Inn, and more.
